How Our Image Compressor Works

Our image compression tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Adjust the compression level slider to your preferred balance of quality and file size
  3. Select your desired output format (or keep the original format)
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds for processing
  5. Download your optimized image

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our image compressor is completely free with no hidden charges or watermarks.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats for both input and output.

Is there a file size limit?

You can compress images up to 10MB in size. For larger files, consider resizing first.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
