Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our image compressor is completely free to use with no hidden charges, watermarks, or registration required.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats for both input and output. You can keep the original format or convert to another format during compression.

Is there a file size limit?

You can compress images up to 10MB in size. For larger files, consider resizing first or using a desktop application.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.
